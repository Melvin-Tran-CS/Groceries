Automatic Warehouse


Warehouse

The process of importing goods into the warehouse through automation.

The automation uses a formula to make sure it finds the average weight of the type of product and then proceeds to move on to the storage process.

Storage

After the import process,it takes specific conditions from a formula different from the first in order to store the goods in a 100% efficient way through a rectangular prism shape.

It takes the dimensions,weight,fragility,and type of product into account when it stocks and stores them together tightly.
